Imitation Learning for Humanoid Robot G1 Manipulation
ETH Robotics Club HACK2026, Mar 2026

This project focused on building a humanoid manipulation pipeline under a 48-hour hackathon setting. We used teleoperation, LeRobot, and an ACT policy for manipulation, and adapted NVIDIA GR00T Whole-Body Control for locomotion.

We collected about 1.5 hours of data across roughly 200 episodes and trained a grasping policy within hours. The final system generalized from a single training object to objects with different sizes and appearances.

High-Frequency Trajectory Tracking for Franka Emika Panda
Personal Project, Feb 2026

I developed a reinforcement learning pipeline in MuJoCo using PPO to enable a 7-DoF Franka Emika Panda robot to track high-frequency figure-8 trajectories.

The project focused on generalization across varied trajectory dimensions and offsets, and also analyzed the mechanical limits of the learned policy to identify safe operating regions.

Reproducibility Study of RL-Based Ramp Metering
Semester Project at ETH Zürich, Oct 2025 - Dec 2025

I implemented ACTM and METANET traffic simulators from scratch in Python and integrated them with custom Q-learning, MPC, and Stable-Baselines3 actor-critic methods.

The project emphasized reproducibility, transparent experimentation, and open-source documentation, with training runs up to 600,000 episodes and strong performance improvements.
